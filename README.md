📊 Key Insights

1️⃣ Revenue Trend
Sales show strong growth towards the end of the year, indicating seasonal demand and higher customer purchases during festive months.

2️⃣ Category Performance
Technology category generates the highest revenue, showing strong demand for tech products.
Furniture and Office Supplies contribute steady but lower revenue.

3️⃣ Top Products
A small group of top products contributes a large portion of total sales.
This shows the Pareto Principle (80/20 rule) in action.

4️⃣ Regional Performance
Some regions generate significantly higher revenue, showing strong market presence.
Low-performing regions present expansion opportunities.


Business Recommendations

1️⃣ Increase Marketing Before Festive Months
Plan promotions before peak sales periods to maximize revenue.

2️⃣ Invest More in Technology Category
Focus inventory and ads on high-performing categories.

3️⃣ Promote Top-Selling Products
Use ads and discounts on top products to boost profit.

4️⃣ Improve Low-Performing Regions
Launch targeted campaigns and offers in weaker regions.
